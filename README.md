## A Case Study on South Korean Demographics 
In this case study, I analyzed South Korea's vital statistics and population. I looked at the growth, decay, and correlations between values to uncover insights, make predictions, and further data-driven decisions. My buisniess tasks include:
- Predict a timeline for population decline
- Identify correlations between certain vital statistics and population

**Role:** Data Analyst  
**Tools:** Google BigQuery (SQL), Google Sheets, Google Slides, Tableau

## Key Skills & Highlights
- Data cleaning and preparation using SQL and Excel
- Exploratory data analysis and pattern discovery
- Drawing actionable insights from complex datasets
- Creating data vizualizations for targeted audiences
- Producing clear, well-documented code for reproducibility

## Key findings include:
- South Korea is experiencing a decline in population
- The rate of decline is accelerating
- The decline may result in a loss of up to 3 million persons by 2032
- There are positive correlations between marriages and important statistics such as birth and total populaiton growth
